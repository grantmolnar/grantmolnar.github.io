About me

I am a AI/ML engineer and an avid mathematician. Professionally, I innovate in areas such as cognitive agents, signal segmentation systems, and satellite scheduling algorithms. Academically, I explore number theory, geometry, graph theory, and game theory.

I received my PhD in mathematics from Dartmouth College in 2023, with John Voight as my advisor. I received my MS in mathematics from Brigham Young University in 2018, with Michael Griffin and Paul Jenkins as my advisors.
